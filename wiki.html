The Journey To West

THE ARCHIVES OF THE EXPANSE

The world is not what it seems, nor is it what it once was. There was a time—long before memory could be trusted—when the land was whole, when the horizon marked the end of sight and not the beginning of uncertainty.

In those distant ages, people lived within boundaries they understood, naming their kingdoms, shaping their histories, and believing that the world obeyed rules that could be learned and preserved. But that certainty began to fade the moment the first journey was taken.

The Westward Expanse

No one remembers who first walked west. No name remains, no record survives. Those who departed never returned, yet something of them lingered. Time lost its strict rhythm. Distance became unreliable. And stories, once fixed and recorded, began to shift depending on who spoke them.

To travel west was not merely to move across land, but to step into a place where reality itself seemed unfinished. The further one journeyed, the less the world resembled what had been left behind. Some travelers claimed to relive moments they had not yet experienced. Others forgot entire portions of their lives as if they had never existed.

Seekers & Keepers

There are those who believe the West holds truth. They call themselves Seekers, embracing the transformation even as it strips them of everything familiar. Opposing them are the Keepers of Origin, who see the West as corruption. They believe identity is sacred and guard the eastern lands against this creeping, unknowable change.

The Inner Power: Essence and Will

Power emerged in the wake of the first journey—intrinsic and deep. It is understood as Essence and Will.

"Essence is what one is at their core—nature made manifest. Will is the strength to remain oneself in a world that constantly erodes identity."
The Hierarchy of Stability
  • The Drifters: Wandering without direction, slowly losing themselves.
  • The Anchored: Possessing a sense of self strong enough to resist the effects of the West.
  • The Awakened: Those who shape their power consciously, bending reality.
  • The Embodied: Beings who have become their Essence, altering the world by their presence.
  • The Transcendent: Reality bends to accommodate them; their actions ripple across time.
The Great Threats

Among the Transcendent reside the greatest mysteries: The Hollow King, seeking to erase all identity; the Flame Ascendant, who believes only the strong deserve to exist; and the Time-Bound Oracle, trapped between moments, seeing unavoidable futures.

The history of this world is written in consequences. The First Journey marked the beginning. The Fracture saw the rise of conflict. And now, somewhere within this shifting reality… a new journey begins.
