The Journey To West

SURVEYOR'S ARCHIVE: THE FRACTURED LANDS

Major States & Regions

The Last Bastion of Order

The Gilded Empire (Chang'an)

The heart of the Eastern Lands where kingdoms rise and fall under established rules. It serves as the primary stronghold for the Keepers of Origin, who guard the remaining order of the world against the creeping change of the West.

The Realm of Transformation

The Westward Expanse

A territory where reality is unfinished and time loses its strict rhythm. Here, Seekers embrace the erosion of identity to evolve their inner Essence, often Reliving moments they have not yet experienced.

High Essence Waste

The Flaming Desert

A harsh landscape where the sun dies and shadows awaken. This region is home to the Flame Ascendant, a Transcendent being who believes the world must be purified through destruction.

Abyssal Kingdom

The Underwater Dragon Palace

A hidden realm where the boundaries of the world are most fluid. It is populated by Embodied beings whose very presence alters the surrounding reality.

Spiritual Origin

The Flower Fruit Mountain

A legendary location within the Expanse where Essence manifests in its purest, most untamed form. It remains a key objective for those seeking to reach the peak of stability as Transcendent beings.
